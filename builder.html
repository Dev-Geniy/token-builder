Vulnerability scanning information

Our tool scans your smart contract for the following vulnerabilities:

  • Number overflow:It is checked that the numbers in the contract do not exceed safe limits, which can lead to execution errors.
  • Unauthorized access They look for hardcoded admin passwords and other weak points that could give attackers access to important contract functions.
  • SQL-injections: If the contract interacts with the database, it is checked for vulnerabilities that could allow attackers to execute arbitrary SQL-queries.

If no vulnerabilities were found, this means that your code is safe from the most common attacks. We made sure that:

  • Your data is protected from overflows and incorrect calculations.
  • The contract contains no known vulnerabilities for unauthorized access.
  • Your transactions are secure and there will be no opportunity for attacking requests.
